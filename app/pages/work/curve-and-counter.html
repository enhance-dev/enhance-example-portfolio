    Honouring the history of Swiss typography

    Curve & Counter is a publication researching the rich history of Swiss typography, commissioned by Typografische Duotsblätter — itself a legendary journal of typography.

    Through interviews and analysis of typographic specimens, Curve & Counter seeks to explore the origins of the Swiss typographic movement, and to uncover its effects upon the typographic industry today.
