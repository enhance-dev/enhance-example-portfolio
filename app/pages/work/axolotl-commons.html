    A place for axolotls to call home

    The Society for the Preservation of the Axolotl (SPA) is a non profit organization fighting to secure a future for the axolotl, a creature sadly on the brink of extinction.

    We worked with the SPA to realize their vision of a multifunctional environment, encompassing research and development facilities in addition to cutting edge housing designed specifically for axolotls.
